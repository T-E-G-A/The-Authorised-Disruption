# The Authorised Disruption

**ABS, AI-Native Law Firms, and the Future of Legal Services in Europe**

A market intelligence report by **Tega Oviasogie** · Largence Group Ltd · June 2026

---

## Overview

This repository contains the source files for *The Authorised Disruption*, a comprehensive market intelligence report examining the regulatory and market conditions for AI-native legal services across seventeen European jurisdictions — the United Kingdom and fifteen EU member states — as of mid-2026.

The report's central argument is that the gap between the United Kingdom and the rest of Europe in AI-native legal services delivery is not a technology gap. It is a **regulatory imagination gap**.

## Analytical Framework

The report applies a four-category firm framework consistently throughout:

| Category | Model | Current Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 01 | ABS Professional Services | Irwin Mitchell, Gateley plc |
| 02 | AI-Platform-as-Law-Firm | Lawhive |
| 03 | AI-Primary — Probabilistic | Garfield.Law |
| 04 | Technology-Only — Deterministic | LawFairy |

Legaltech platforms (Harvey, Legora, Clio) are tracked separately as market readiness signals. They sell tools to the existing profession without delivering regulated legal services directly and do not belong in the firm framework.
